Illegal Trafficking and Poaching

The poaching and trafficking of animal parts is the second most profitable crime in the world. Making an estimated 23 billion dollar yearly profit, a third of that comes from bear parts which are commonly supplied from Canada.

Factory Farming Meat Industry

Factory farming is a horrible industry that allows the exploitation and abuse of animals. Spending their lives in a building crowded by thousands of other chickens, chickens suffer immense trauma. Their beaks are clipped off as chicks, they are genetically mutated to row and die faster, and most are fed drugs to keep them from being sicker than they already are.

Animal Testing

Rabbits are one of the primary animals who are used in laboratories for cosmetic product testing. They spend their lives in small cages, having chemicals plastered into their eyes, mouth, and skin. They suffer from PTSD, depression and have horrible rashes that spread throughout their skin from the testing. Most go blind and die from maltreatment.

Factory Farming Dairy Industry

Cows in the dairy industry are being constantly abused and mistreated. Starting from birth, females are artificially inseminated to spend the rest of their lives giving birth and producing milk. Most cows die from disease, exhaustion, or depression. Male calves are taken from their mothers and taken to factories to be killed and sold as veal.
